Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply for incubation?

Any current student or recent alumnus of Techno India University with a scalable business idea can apply. Your idea can be at any stage—from "napkin sketch" to MVP. We help refine it from there.

Do you take equity in the startup?

Our primary goal is educational. Equity requirements depend on the level of funding and resource intensity provided. For most early-stage startups in our program, we don't take equity.

Can I apply with just an idea?

Absolutely! Yes, we help move ideas from "paper" to "product." Our ideation and scoping phase is specifically designed to validate and refine your concept before development begins.

How long is the incubation program?

The program typically spans 6-12 months, but it's flexible based on your startup's needs and progress. We move at your pace while keeping you accountable to milestones.

Is there funding available?

We don't provide direct funding, but we connect you with our network of angel investors, VCs, and grant programs. Successful pitch events often lead to seed funding opportunities.

Our Journey

Futurepreneurs E-Cell: Annual Report 2025

A comprehensive record of entrepreneurial initiatives and events throughout the 2025 academic session.

Idea Ignition 1.0

Battle of Business Pitches

Aug 21, 2025

Inter-Collegiate Business Pitch Competition powered by Eureka!, IIT Bombay. Participants pitched ideas in 3+1 minute windows followed by 2-minute Q&A sessions with industry experts from 3S Travel Network and Mikata Consulting. Judged on innovation, feasibility, market potential, and presentation clarity.

Impact: Motivated students to transition from "job seekers" to "job creators" mindset.

Vision Vault

Online Social Media Challenge

Sep 7-9, 2025

High-engagement digital pitching competition centered on "One Minute. One Reel. One Big Idea." Participants conceptualized scalable business ideas within finite budget constraints using the hashtag #PitchItWithFuturepreneurs.

Impact: Trained students in concise communication, digital pitching, and financial feasibility evaluation with extensive social media reach.

Soch Samvaad

Reimagining the Entrepreneurial Ecosystem

Sep 11, 2025

Entrepreneurial group discussion addressing three core pillars: Freelancing as a Career, Problem-Solving vs. Funding, and Frugal Innovation. Focused on institutional support for alternative career paths and problem-first ideation.

Strategic Outcome: Resolved to advocate for institutional recognition of freelancing and mentor startups with focus on social relevance.

Illuminate Unveiled 2025

Entrepreneurship Workshop

Oct 11, 2025

Exclusive workshop in collaboration with E-Cell IIT Bombay featuring keynote speaker Dr. Rohini Sharma on innovation mindsets, business model design, and startup growth strategies. Interactive brainstorming and prototyping sessions included.

Recognition: Futurepreneurs recognized among Top 100 colleges nationwide by IIT Bombay. Vision: Entrepreneurship as meaningful change through courage and collaboration.

Zenith Launchpad 2025

Professional Conclave

Nov 22, 2025

Premier bridge between academic learning and startup ecosystem with 13 diverse industry leaders. Inaugural address by Prof. (Dr.) Samiran Chattopadhyay on resilience and innovation. Speakers shared "Journey Chronicles" from food ventures, nursery management, and brand development. Exclusive in-depth interview with Mr. Sunando Banerjee on business leadership and operational excellence.

Impact: Grassroots insights from industry leaders and emphasis on practical, real-world business acumen.
